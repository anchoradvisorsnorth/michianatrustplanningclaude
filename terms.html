4Description of Services

Michiana Trust Planning provides automated document preparation services. After you complete our intake questionnaire and submit payment, we generate personalized estate planning documents based on your responses. Depending on the package purchased, documents may include:

  • Indiana Revocable Living Trust
  • Pour-Over Will
  • Certificate of Trust
  • Durable Financial Power of Attorney
  • Health Care Representative Designation and Living Will
  • Assignment of Personal Property
  • Quitclaim Deed to Trust

All documents are delivered in PDF format via email. Document bundles include a Notarization Checklist and Asset Funding Guide specific to Indiana.

Documents are not legally effective until properly signed, witnessed (where required), and notarized in accordance with Indiana law. Receiving your documents does not mean your trust is created — execution and funding are your responsibility.
